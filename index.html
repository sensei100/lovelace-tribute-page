Maud Hart Lovelace

Maud Hart Lovelace is the author of the Betsy-Tacy books. Set in Deep Valley, Minnesota, the books follow the lives of Betsy Ray and Tacy Kelly as they grow up.

The Mankato public library's children's room is named in honor of Mrs. Lovelace and the Minnesota Youth Reading Award is named after her as well. You can find out more about her at the Betsy-Tacy Society.
